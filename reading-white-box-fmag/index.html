Reading White Box: FMAG

21 May, 2021

White Box: Fantastic Medieval Adventure Game (WB: FMAG) is a tabletop roleplaying game by Charlie Mason that comes from the Old School Revival (OSR) sub-genre of RPGs. It traces its history through two different clones of original Dungeons and Dragons - Swords and Wizardry and Swords and Wizardry: Whitebox. The PDF version is available for free.

I recently purchased a physical copy of the game (the “red” cover for anyone curious) and have been slowly reading through it. I figured I should take advantage of my shiny new website to post my reading notes, observations and possible house rules.
